The Invariant now has an address of its own.

For the longest time, the site used a github.io address. That was enough for then but not now.

The address has changed to [theinvariant.uk](https://theinvariant.uk).

The new domain does not change the work. It gives the work a name under which to gather.

The site will remain quiet. In time, the work may make its own way.

The contact: [contact@theinvariant.uk](mailto:contact@theinvariant.uk).

The site has reached the new present. History is becoming, its shape, only glimpsed.
