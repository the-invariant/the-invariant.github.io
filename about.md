# About

The Invariant is a place for thinking aloud about science, technology, minds, bodies, machines, stories, and intelligence.

The starting point might be a film, a comic, a biology paper, a question from psychology or neuroscience, a piece of software, or a machine that seems worth understanding a little better.

Mostly this is a notebook for following ideas carefully and seeing where they lead.
